Web Design & Development

Tiny Vinyl Shop

A local record shop needed a web presence that matched its personality. I designed and built the full site from scratch using HTML, CSS, and vanilla JS. Working with the playful brand the owner had created for the shop, I used it to inform the design.

The goal was simple: make it easy for customers to find the shop, get a feel for what it has to offer, and walk through the door. The design leans into the analog aesthetic of vinyl itself, considered typography, unhurried layout, and details that reward a second look.
